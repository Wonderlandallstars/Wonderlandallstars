What to Expect

Athletes will be evaluated on tumbling, jumps, motions, flexibility, performance, and attitude.

Elite Team Tumbling Requirements

Level 1

Standing (4 passes):

  • 3 back walkovers
  • Cartwheel + 2 back walkovers
  • Round-off back walkover
  • Choice pass

Running (4 passes):

  • Round-off back handspring

Level 2

Standing (4 passes):

  • Standing back handspring
  • Back handspring + back walkover
  • Two back handsprings
  • Choice pass

Running (4 passes):

  • Round-off back handspring
  • Round-off back handspring tuck

Level 3

Standing (4 passes):

  • Two back handsprings
  • Back handspring tuck
  • Standing tuck
  • Choice pass

Running (4 passes):

  • Round-off back handspring tuck
  • Layouts encouraged

Level 4+

Standing (4 passes):

  • Standing tuck
  • Standing series to tuck
  • Whip connections
  • Choice pass

Running (4 passes):

  • Layouts
  • Fulls / specialty passes encouraged

All athletes should show clean technique and safe execution.
