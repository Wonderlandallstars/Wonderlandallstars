Elite Team Tumbling Format

All elite athletes perform 4 standing and 4 running tumbling passes.

Level 1

  • Standing: 3 Back Walkovers, Cartwheel + 2 Back Walkovers, Bridge Kickover
  • Running: Round-off, Round-off rebound, Round-off back walkover

Level 2

  • Standing: Back Handspring, Series attempt, Back Walkover + BHS
  • Running: Round-off BHS, RO BHS series

Level 3

  • Standing: BHS Series, BHS Tuck
  • Running: RO BHS Tuck, RO BHS series

Level 4

  • Standing: BHS Tuck, Standing Combo
  • Running: RO BHS Tuck, Combo pass

Level 5

  • Standing: Full, Specialty combo
  • Running: Full, Combination pass

Level 6
