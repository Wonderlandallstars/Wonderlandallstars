Elite Season Expectations

Commitment. Discipline. Championship Mindset.

Attendance Policy

Attendance is mandatory. Athletes are expected to maintain 95% attendance. Excessive absences may result in routine position changes or removal from competition. No missed practices the week of competition.

Practice & Extra Training

Additional practices may be scheduled before competitions. Required tumbling classes must be maintained for skill progression. Crossovers must meet all team requirements.

Competition Travel

Families are responsible for all travel accommodations. Athletes must stay with team hotel blocks when required. Full weekend availability is expected.

Athlete Conduct

Respect toward coaches, teammates, and competitors is non-negotiable. Negative attitudes, drama, or unsportsmanlike behavior will not be tolerated. Social media must reflect positive representation of the program.

Parent Conduct

Parents are expected to uphold professionalism at competitions and within the gym. Disrespect toward staff or athletes may result in dismissal from the program.

Financial Commitment

Tuition, competition fees, choreography, uniforms, and travel costs must be paid on time. Failure to meet financial obligations may result in suspension from practice or competition.
